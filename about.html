About Us

INSTITUTE

The institute was established in the year 1997 following approval of All India Council for Technical Education. The institute offers Undergraduate and Postgraduate teaching programs in various disciplines of science and technology. It is a leading institution of central India. The institute has adopted internationally accepted Continuous Evaluation along with Credit and Grade Point Assessment System of evaluation for awarding the degrees. The institute was also made its mark as an emerging center for research in some key areas in engineering and technology including Antenna technology Nanotechnology, Communication technology, VLSI, Data mining, image processing, IT, product designing etc.

TEKNISKA 2019

TEKNISKA-2019 is expected to bring students from all technical, social, science and professional streams together on a common platform to show their technical as well as latent energy. We have overwhelming response already and looking forward for participation from all over the state.
We wish to make this Tech-fest an absolutely memorable event for all visitors. Main features of TEKNIKSA-2019 includes Paper presentation, Robotics, Bridge making, Yuva sansad, Kavi sammelan etc. The three days Tech-fest is expected to bring exclusive audience to all the venue with more than 800 footfalls.
